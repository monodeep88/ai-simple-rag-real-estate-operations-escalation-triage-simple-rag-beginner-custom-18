VENDOR PAYMENT POLICY - V1.2
Effective Date: 2023-11-01

1. Invoice Submission
All vendor invoices must be submitted within 30 days of service completion. Invoices should include a detailed description of services, date of service, and work order number.

2. Payment Terms
Standard payment terms are Net 30 days from the invoice date. Expedited payments must be pre-approved by the Property Manager or Regional Director.

3. Overdue Invoices
- 31-45 Days Overdue: Accounts Payable (AP) sends an automated reminder to the vendor and notifies the Operations Coordinator.
- 46-60 Days Overdue: Operations Coordinator reviews the invoice history, contacts the vendor for resolution, and flags the invoice for Property Manager review.
- 61+ Days Overdue: Property Manager reviews, potentially escalates to Regional Director for resolution, and considers temporary suspension of vendor services if no resolution is reached.

4. Invoice Disputes
Any disputed invoices must be reported to Accounts Payable within 10 days of receipt. Disputes should include clear reasons and supporting documentation.
